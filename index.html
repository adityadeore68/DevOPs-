Advanced Portal Features

Discover the powerful features that make our Crime Investigation Portal the most effective solution for law enforcement and public safety

Multi-Level Authentication

Secure role-based access control with two-factor authentication for citizens, police officers, and administrators.

Digital Complaint & FIR System

SDLC Model: Waterfall

This project follows the structured Waterfall Model of Software Development Life Cycle with clearly defined phases:
